GCER Update

(7.18.2019) With 399 reunited with 742, we headed off to Norman, OK for GCER. After two years of 3rd place, we finally were able to get it done and win. The. Whole. Competition.
Here's a list of all we won:

-Overall World Champion
-1st Place Double Elimination (undefeated too...)
-1st Place Double Seeding
-2nd Place Seeding
-Overall Judges Choice Award

Here we are with all of our trophies after the award ceremony:
Thank you to Victor Wang for hosting and coaching us this year. We would not have been successful without his influence and support.

We're also saying goodbye to Team Captain and Head of Building Oscar Bjorkman as he graduates from the program. We are sad to see him go and he will be missed.

WE ARE THE CHAMPIONS! (And 2nd Place)

(4.13.2019) 399 and 742 took big dubs this weekend as they finished 1st and second overall in the regional seeding competition. The two teams will be breaking for about a month and will plan to reform into one super team for GCER. Here is a list of all of our accolades:

-1st in Seeding (399)
-2nd in Seeding (742)
-1st in Double Elimination (399)
-3rd in Double Elimination (742)
-1st Overall (399)
-2nd Overall (742)
-KISS (Keep It Simple, Students) Award (399)
-Outstanding Documentation (742)

Thank you to all the parents and mentors who helped us this season and a special thanks to Victor Wang for graciously hosting the team at his house.
