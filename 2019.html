WE ARE THE CHAMPIONS! (And 2nd Place)

399 and 742 took big dubs this weekend as they finished 1st and second overall in the regional seeding competition. The two teams will be breaking for about a month and will plan to reform into one super team for GCER. Here is a list of all of our accolades:

-1st in Seeding (399)
-2nd in Seeding (742)
-1st in Double Elimination (399)
-3rd in Double Elimination (742)
-1st Overall (399)
-2nd Overall (742)
-KISS (Keep It Simple, Students) Award (399)
-Outstanding Documentation (742)
