2018: PARTS



We are a group of bay area teens vying for a top spot at this year's GCER competition. When we aren't meeting from 7-10pm most nights, we frequent our local Pho Vi Hoa, a restaurant our team has essentially made a second meeting place. Every year, during our season commencement dinner, we decide on a theme for our team name and robot names. This involves intense brainstorming which usually ends indecisively and is the topic of discussion during our meetings for the following weeks. This year we decided on PARTS. PARTS was intended to be an acronym describing our team — perhaps “Palo Alto Robotics”? After coming to the realization that the acronym would be scarily similar to the ITEMS of last year, we dropped it. But we kept everything else, and we have adopted it as our identity. We are PARTS.

Our 2018 Season So Far:
-Regional Overall Champion
-Regional Top Seeding Score
-Regional Double Elimination Champion
-Regional Outstanding Documentation Award


Wish us luck and we'll see you all at GCER!
