2018: PARTS

So you've decided to come to our website. You may have been about to search for lactose intolerance remedies, or you may have decided to learn more about our team. Either way, while you're here, we should fill you in on our team. ...haha shameless advertising...

So who are we?

We are a group of bay area teens vying for a top spot at this year's here competition. When we aren't meeting from 7-10pm most nights, we frequent our local Pho Vi Hoa, a restaurant our team has essentially made a second meeting place. Every year, during our season commencement dinner, we decide on a theme for our team name and robot names. This involves intense brainstorming which usually ends indecisively and is the topic of discussion during our meetings for the following weeks. This year we decided on PARTS. PARTS was intended to be an acronym describing our team — perhaps “Palo Alto Robotics”? After coming to the realization that the acronym would be scarily similar to the ITEMS of last year, we dropped it. But we kept everything else, and we have adopted it as our identity. We are PARTS.

Our team is cheered on by our captain's cat, Stars, our unofficial team mascot. Stars is joined by a group of excellent parents who support us through transportation, meals, part orders, and the general emotional support that only a parent can provide when the robot stops working....

We owe the existence of our team to the Los Altos Community Robotics conglomorate, an informal community organisation dedicated to youth robotics in our area. They are run completely by volunteer parents who decided to take time out of their busy days to help us. Thank you so much parents for your undying support for our team on an organisational and personal level. We would not be where we are without their tremendous help.

Our 2018 Season So Far:
-Regional Overall Champion
-Regional Top Seeding Score
-Regional Double Elimination Champion
-Regional Outstanding Documentation Award


Wish us luck and we'll see you all at GCER!
